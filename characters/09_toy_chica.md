# Toy Chica

- Click on her cupcake if you see it in your office.
- If Nightmarrione is active, the cupcake will never on the far side that Nightmarrione is on.
- The cupcake is always centered if AI $\leq$ 5.
    - The cupscake appears in the center three positions if $5 < \text{AI} \leq 10$.
    - Otherwise, the cupcake appears in 1 of the 5 possible positions.
- The cupcake can decide to spawn when the camera is up, **but only actually spawns when the camera is lowered (meaning the timer doesn't start until the monitor is lowered).** The higher the AI, the more likely the cupcake will be spawned.
- The moment the cupcake is spawned when the camera is lowered, it will not spawn again for another 15 seconds
- The audio is panned. Wearing headphones will help.
- You have `15-(AI/2)` seconds to click on the cupcake after it has spawned. That is 5 seconds on AI 20.
