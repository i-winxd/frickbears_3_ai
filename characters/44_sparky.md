# Sparky

- Spawns after 120 to 30 seconds depending on AI.
- Never spawns where Mangle is.
  - The reverse does not apply. Mangle may move to a camera Sparky is on.
- Must be found from 60 to 25 seconds based on AI.
  - This ticks down twice as fast if the cameras are up and the monitor is on "cameras."
  - Immediately lose 1 second of this timer every time  you switch a camera.
