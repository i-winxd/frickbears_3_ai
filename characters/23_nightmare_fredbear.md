# Nightmare Fredbear

- Effectively reduces the maximum temperature you can withstand.
  - The game's temperature limit is 40.
  - Nightmare Fredbear activates when the temperature is `30 - AI`, effectively quartering the limit at AI 20.
- Beyond a temperature threshold, Fredbear will close his jaws on you. They will only rise if the temperature falls below that.
- A jumpscare occurs if the jaws are too low.
- Power AC (temperature can be negative) can be really helpful.
