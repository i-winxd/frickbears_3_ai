# Twisted Wolf

- Never appears with Withered Bonnie or Shadow Freddy
- Every time the camera is flipped up, there is a 10/35 to 10/15 chance Twisted wolf will appear in your office when you flip the camera down.
  - If this roll fails, Twisted Wolf will not spawn
- This roll only occurs when he is not in your office. If it has been less than 5 seconds since he left, the roll will be delayed until that time.
- Only attacks when the camera is put down
- You have 5 to 2 seconds to react depending on AI.
