# Nightmare Freddy

- There are 9 possible Freddle locations
- Each Freddle operates independently but they can spawn in waves. The higher the AI, the more they spawn at a time.
  - If a Freddle exists for too long, it attaches a Freddle (called a "facehugger") to your face before despawning. **This timer is not impacted by AI** and is 10 seconds.
- If there are two facehuggers and another one would've spawned, you get a jumpscare.
- Facehuggers do not despawn. Once they are there, they will remain until you get jumpscared.

Time between Freddle spawns (there is a delay between spawning Freddles)

A wave spawns Freddles. Waves occur every 20 to 10 seconds depending on AI.
