# Golden Freddy

- Will never appear if Shadow Freddy or Twisted Wolf is in your office. Note that Twisted Wolf spawns differently - the game decides if Twisted Wolf is to appear before deciding that of Golden Freddy.
- Spawns in your office with a probability of $\text{AI}/20$ every time the camera is flipped down if he isn't there already
- You have 2 seconds to put on your mask when he spawns. This is unaffected by his AI level. It doesn't. The mask only needs to be down for one frame.
