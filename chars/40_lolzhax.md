# Lolzhax

- The animatronic that is summoned inherits Lolzhax's AI level.
- Running out of time will auto-select the random option.
- Can only summon animatronics not present. If there are 2 or less animatronics that can be summoned, the window will only display 0. If every animatronic has at least AI level 1, LolzHax will not appear.
