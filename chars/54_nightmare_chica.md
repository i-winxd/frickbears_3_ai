# Nightmare Chica

- Advances every 38 to 18 seconds depending on AI, multiplied by 0.75 to 1.25 randomly each time
- Once advancing, Nightmare Chica turns her head after `300-AI*2` frames and turns it back after `430+AI*2` frames (from when she starts advancing). The game runs at 60 frames per second.
- When she is looking at you and your mask isn't on for 0.5 seconds, it's a jumpscare. Putting your mask on resets this timer.
