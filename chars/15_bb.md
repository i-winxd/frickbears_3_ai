# BB

- Is never lured by the audio lure
- 0.5 move speed

- Normally disables your flashlight, but will jumpscare you when he would normally enter:
  - If none of the other characters are "lethal"
  - If you're playing on Lunatic difficulty (double check if it's hard, checks if difficulty `== 3`)
  - Otherwise with a probability of $\frac{\text{night no.}^2}{100}$.
- Flashlight disable duration is always 30 seconds


Please refer to [Toy Bonnie's page](./08_toy_bonnie.md) for vent animatronic mechanics.
