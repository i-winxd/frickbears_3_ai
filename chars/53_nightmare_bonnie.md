# Nightmare Bonnie

- Advances every 36 to 12 seconds depending on AI, multiplied by 0.75 to 1.25 randomly each time
- Picks a random side when attacking
- Plays clattering sound on approach, gaining when he's closer while attacking (based on how close he is to jumpscaring)
- Any time he is attacking, the window to use the flashlight is on. Just flash him.
- This window is between 7 to 4 seconds depending on AI
