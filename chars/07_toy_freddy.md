# Toy Freddy

- Beat Toy Freddy's high score in a minigame of your choice before 6AM.
- If the night is shorter (starts at a later time), his required scores will scale proportionaly to the length of the night.
- At SP, scores are based on him being at AI level 10.
- Required scores are linearly interpolated (at 0 percent for AI 0 and 100 percent for AI 20):
  1. Fredsweeper: 120s to 45s
  2. Air Adventure (flappy bird): 5 to 25
  3. Chomping with Chica: 0 to 100
  4. Puppet Patrol: 10 to 30
  5. Hare Pares: 360s to 60s
  6. TBA: 5 to 25
  7. TBA: 10 to 30
  8. TBA: 360 (always)
  9. TBA: 5 to 20
  10. Cupcake Clicker: 50 to 200
  11. TBA: 20 to 100
