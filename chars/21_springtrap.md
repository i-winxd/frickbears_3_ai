# Springtrap

- Moves every `(lerp(42, 12, AI / 20) * RandomValue * 60) + 300;` frames (game runs at 60FPS)
- Flashlight must be held longer at higher AI levels
- Parts and service $\to$ showstage $\to$ left hallway $\to$ kill stage
- Once at kill stage, cannot be pushed back by the flashlight and your only option is to hold the left door closed for the rest of the night
