# Plushtrap

- **Always appears in the window after lowering the monitor regardless of AI level, unless he has already done so and has not reset.**
- Never appears if the Laser doors are active. If the Laser doors were activated before, it is the same as if a flashlight was always flashing Plushtrap at the correct door.
- If Twisted wolf is present, Plushtrap always moves to the **right.**
- Always jumpscares after a 0.17s delay if the wrong side is flashed AND the doors are not closed on the side he is on (you cannot stop this at this phase).
- The flashlight only needs to be on for one frame. This means you can deal with Plushtrap even when Endo is up.
- **Plushtrap's animation plays faster the higher is AI level,** making him harder to see.
- After the animation finishes, that is when the window to flash plushtrap starts. In this window:
  - You have `5 - ((AI / 20) * 3)` seconds to react.
  - When the door he is on is closed, it effectively **pauses** his jumpscare timer (time to react). However this also means you can't flash him.
