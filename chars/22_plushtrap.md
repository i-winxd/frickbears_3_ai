# Plushtrap

- Always jumpscares after a 0.5s delay if the wrong side is flashed AND the doors are not closed on the side he is on.
- When the door he is on is closed, it effectively **pauses** his jumpscare timer. However this also means you can't flash him.
- Jumpscare timer is `5 - ((AI / 20) * 3)` in seconds.
