# Nightmarionne

- There doesn't seem to be anything preventing Nightmarionne from spawning after Dreadbear or Toy Chica's cupcake has spawned. This is contrary to the inverse, where IF Nightmarionne is already there, Dreadbear won't request candy on the side Nightmarionne is in.
- Lasts 4 to 8 seconds depending on AI, linearly.
- Shows up after `(45 - (AI * 1.5))` multiplied by uniform 0.8 to 1.2 from the initinal state.
-  You are allowed to look at Nightmarionne for a small period of time (2.5 to 1.25 seconds depending on AI) (this accumulates for the duration he is down). This time lowers the higher the AI level.
