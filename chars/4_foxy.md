# Foxy

- Foxy has an internal charge timer. It is 345  seconds on AI 1 and 60 seconds on AI 20; anything between is linearly interpolated. Unlike Freddy, Bonnie, and Chica, there is no random variation in these movement times. This is how long it takes for him to "charge."
    - If cameras are not used at all, and the camera isn't focused on him, he charges up as normal.
    - If the camera is up, **or** the camera is down and on him, he charges up at half speed.
    - Otherwise, if the camera is up and focused on him, he does not charge up at all.
    - His visibility on the camera depends on the charge.
- Once that charges up, you will hear a running sound and have 1.5 seconds to close the left door. If you don't, it's a jumpscare.
- Foxy drains 1 percent the first time you block him, 2 percent the next time, 3, then 4, then 5. It caps at 5.
- Foxy resets after being blocked.
- If Marionette is inactive and you haven't opened your cameras for 60 seconds, Foxy will continuously run over and over again, as an anti-camping mechanic.
