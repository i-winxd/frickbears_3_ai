# Temperature

- The limit is 40. Reach that and it's game over.
- Temperature naturally increases by 0.5 per second when the fan is off.
- With the fan on, temperature decreases by `FanSpeed * 2` per second.

- Fan Speed:
  - 1 by default
  - 2 with the tier 1 fan upgrade
  - 5 with the tier 2 fan upgrade

Make sure to allocate 30% of your power to the fan, maybe less if you can end the night early or end with high temperature. The Fan Speed might be worth it over overcharge.
